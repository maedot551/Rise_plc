AboutUs

RISE consulting Architects and engineers is a grade five architectural and construction services plc. located in Addis Ababa. For 1 year, our company has provided services in the Educational, Commercial, and Corporate, Industrial, Healthcare and Residential markets throughout Ethiopia. To better serve our clients and improve our control over cost, scheduling and design, our services include: Architecture, Interior Design, Architectural Construction Services, Preservation, Sustainability and planning the firm made its duty is to deliver exceptional design ideas and solutions for our clients through the creative blending of human need, environmental stewardship, value creation, science and art. Our design solutions result from a collaborative process that encourages multidisciplinary professional teams to research alternatives, share knowledge and imagine new ways to solve the challenges of the built environment. Our staff of 10 full-time architects, Engineers, interns, interior designers, and construction personnel... all focused on 'people' and 'life'.


Vision

To be recognized as an architecture engineering and planning firm that provides the highest quality of services with integrity and professionalism in protecting the revenue of Ethiopia, facilitating system, enforcing laws and fulfilling international obligations and thinking


Mission

To perform our duties efficiently using the best management, intelligence and technology. To deliver quality service on the principles of facilitation, accountability, consistency, transparency and simplicity. To develop an organizational culture that recognizes the importance of staff contribution and commitment.
